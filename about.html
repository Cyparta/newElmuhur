    About Company

    Elmuhur Organic & Helathy Foods Provider Farming

    El-Muhur Company for Food Industries established its factory in Gaziantep in 2017, when the company started producing one item at that time, which is (date syrup) and began exporting its product to the State of Iraq only. After that, the company developed its machines and produced other items such as jams and tomato paste. The company's management changed in 2021 and began to develop its products and human cadres, as the number of its products reached 40 products, including jams of all kinds, vinegar of all kinds, canned mushrooms, tomato paste, pepper sauce and other food products, as our products have obtained Turkish and international health and food certificates
